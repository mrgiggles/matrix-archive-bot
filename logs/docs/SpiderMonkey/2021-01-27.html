nbp: Well, I should sort of correct myself. If you have a stream, and you call getReader() on it, that reader has a .ready property. That property is a getter that returns a Promise, and you can .then() on it as many times as you want. Ideally, you only .then once, and that single time will do work that would read a new value of the .ready property (a distinct/different/separate Promise) and then .then() on it to keep going.

Seems like that sort of error would break the page entirely, but at least it's not entirely inconceivable.
